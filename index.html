Choices

  • Zoom on hover

    When you hover on a thumbnail image, you can see a magnified version of the image on the right hand side

  • Pop over of ratings

    When you hover on ratings of a product, you can see a popover showing the different ratings of that product. You can then move the cursor to the pop over and then hover it on top of the 5 stars or the 4 stars link and you see a tooltip describing the percentage of ratings

  • Pop over menu with submenu

    There's menu anchor and you can pop over on it to get a submenu. Further poping over on the submenu again shows submenu for the submenu
